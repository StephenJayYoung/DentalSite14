Services We Offer

Family Care

Family Care


From toddlers to the elderly, our skilled dental team is familiar with the unique needs of every patient, no matter their age. Our goal is to help them develop great oral habits that will last them a lifetime.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Click for more information

Cosmetic Dentistry


Cosmetic dentistry is one of the most cost-effective ways to improve your appearance. A beautiful, bright, healthy smile can increase self confidence and overall life satisfaction.

Implant Dentistry

Click for more information

Implant Dentistry


Dental implants are a great option for replacing missing teeth, improving chewing ability, eliminating bridges, and replacing dentures. Call us today for a complimentary implant consultation.

Implant-Supported Dentures

Click for more information

Implant-Supported Dentures


Do you wear dentures or have a mouthful of missing or failing teeth? Eat what you want again, smile, laugh, and look and feel years younger with our All-on-4™ same-day implant-supported dentures. Call us for a free implant consultation.

Invisalign® Orthodontics

Click for more information

Invisalign® Orthodontics


These “invisible” aligners are the latest in orthodontic technology for adults and teens. Virtually undetectable, get the beautiful smile you’ve always wanted.

Crowns & Bridges

Click for more information

Crowns & Bridges


Protect and keep badly decayed or fractured teeth while maintaining a beautiful smile. Made to perfectly match the shape and shade of your other teeth, they’re durable, strong, and undetectable.

Veneers & Bonding

Click for more information

Veneers & Bonding


Veneers are very thin porcelain pieces that are custom made to adhere directly to the front of teeth without grinding or shaving. Bonding is a low-cost way to improve your smile with the use of a tooth-colored composite resin. Transform your smile without drilling on your natural teeth!

Oral Cancer Screening

Click for more information

Oral Cancer Screening


To detect oral cancer at a curable stage, we routinely screen our patients using the ViziLite Plus® system to ensure you’re healthy. Since early detection is the key to increasing the odds of fighting this cancer, make an appointment today.

Periodontal Therapy

Click for more information

Periodontal Therapy


Gum disease is treated by carefully removing the bacteria and substances that form in the pockets around the teeth. The removal of this material occurs on a microscopic level and requires great skill. Our dental team has had advanced training to effectively remove all of the bacteria.
